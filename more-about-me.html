Hobbies & Free Time

Outside of code and coursework, I recharge by playing basketball, getting lost in books, or exploring new languages. Each activity offers something different; movement, reflection, and perspective. It helps me stay connected to myself beyond the screen.

I gravitate toward books that leave a mark, like The Forty Rules of Love, which opened my heart to spiritual depth and human connection, or Atomic Habits, which reshaped how I think about growth, consistency, and the power of small actions. I read with both heart and intention, because I believe real growth happens when we develop both our mindset and our inner world.
